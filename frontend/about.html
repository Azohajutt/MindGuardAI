About MindGuardAI

Revolutionizing mental health risk assessment through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

MindGuardAI is an advanced mental health risk assessment tool that leverages machine learning to provide early insights into mental wellness. Our mission is to make mental health assessment accessible, anonymous, and actionable for everyone.

How It Works

Our AI model, trained on over 25,000 data points, analyzes 24 different factors including lifestyle habits, stress indicators, psychological patterns, and medical history to predict mental health risk levels with 94% accuracy.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Flask, Python, Scikit-learn
  • Model: Random Forest Classifier with SMOTE balancing
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Chart.js
  • Deployment: Flask Development Server

Privacy Guarantee

We take your privacy seriously. No personal data is ever stored on our servers. All assessments are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after results are generated.

Important Note

MindGuardAI is a screening tool and not a diagnostic instrument. If you're experiencing mental health challenges, please consult with a qualified mental health professional.
